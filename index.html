SOUTH EAST LONDON SOCIAL RUNNING GROUP

  • About us

Since moving to South East London in 2018 we've struggled to find a
running group that offers a good work out but is social too, so we thought
why not create our own!

Come join us for our weekly evening run and maybe a drink or two
afterwards! We meet at Joyce in Brockley (next to Co-op) at 7.15pm every
Thursday with us running in 2 groups, group 1 meet outside Joyce and run at
approx. 6 - 6:30 minutes per km and run 6 - 7 km, group 2 meet outside
L'Oculto and run at approx. 5 - 5:30 minutes per km and run around 7 - 10 km.
To keep up to date, you can get in touch with us on Instagram or Facebook,

We run some other more informal sessions throughout the week, so if you
want to get involed just reach out!
